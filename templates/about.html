About

This website is a school project for COSC 381 at Eastern Michigan University made by Athena Xia.

The main functions are Search, Feedback, and Settings.

Search:
Search the descriptions of YouTube videos contained in the index. Select a topic and enter a search term to display results. Each search will be recorded in a database and can be viewed by clicking the link above the search bar. In the Search History page, past queries can be deleted from the database. Users can opt out of having their searches recorded via the Settings page.

Feedback:
Users can leave feedback for the website by entering a subject and message into the appropriate fields. The email field is optional and, if filled in, will send a copy of the user's message to the indicated email address.

Settings:
The two settings are search history and timezone. Search history can be toggled on and off. Timezone can be changed to one of the four most prevalent timezones found in the U.S. and will affect the Date/Time column in Search History.
